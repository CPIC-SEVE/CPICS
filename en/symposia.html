Symposia

Karen Cristine Goncalves dos Santos





The Annual International Symposium of Center SÈVE is a scientific meeting which reflects CSIPC’s philosophy. Its objective is to develop Center SÈVE’s international network and, at the same time, give a chance to student members to meet international researcher. This is an opportunity to increase Center SÈVE’s visibility at international scene but also to create new collaborations between Canadians and international researchers. Student members take advantage of this privileged event to present their own research in front of their peers, enjoying the proximity favored by the event.
